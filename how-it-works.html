The Process

Four steps.
Perfect garden.

No guesswork, no wasted plants. Intelligent data working for your green space.

01

Detect Your Location & Live Weather

Select from 160+ Indian cities. ClimateGuard instantly fetches live data from OpenWeatherMap including temperature, humidity, rainfall, and sky conditions to precisely model your growing environment.

02

AI Matches Plants to Your Climate Profile

Your climate data is compared against scientifically defined tolerance thresholds for every plant in our database. Temperature ranges, humidity requirements, and rainfall profiles are cross-referenced to surface plants that will genuinely thrive.

03

Gemini AI Image Optimizer

Upload any photo of your space. Gemini AI analyses the scene, places photorealistic plants suited to that environment, then identifies each one with its common name, description, and care tips in seconds.

04

Preview Plants in Augmented Reality

Before buying anything, see any of our 80+ 3D plant models placed in your real environment using your smartphone camera via WebXR. Walk around them, check scale, confirm the fit.
